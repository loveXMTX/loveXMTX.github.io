作文WRITING

写作的思维导图如下：

Source

T1 33%

T2 66%

Task 1

Requirements
  • 150 words
  • 20 min
  • Chart
  • Be objective，no subjective
Structure
  • Introduction
    • must be very clear
    • rewrite the prompt and/or add a bit of essential info
    • do not copy prompt
  • Overview
    • most important
    • Summary, no conclusion
    • signposting phrase “Overall”/“On the whole”
    • include comparisons
    • capture 2-3 key features
    • no including specific data or number
  • Body1
  • Body2
    • 2 paragraphs
    • include comparisons
    • include specific data or number
Timetables

  • 审题 1min

    注意年份，时态

  • 写 18min

  • 检查 1min

Preparation

  • 掌握结构

  • 背词汇/句式

    小作文句式pdf

Task2

1、Task response

  • 一定要address all issues
  • 必须entirely on topic
  • 观点和事例之间必须吻合
  • Words Suggest 270-320

2、Coherence& cohesion

  • 4-5 paragraph

  • One idea one paragraph

  • 善用连词

    • variety
    • accuracy

  • Paragraph Structure

    Point, Elaboration, Example, Point

3、Lexical Resource

  • 一定要用对固定搭配collocations
  • 尽量避免重复（概念词除外）
  • 不要有拼写错误
  • 不要用非正式语言
    • It is
    • It’s
    • I am
    • I’m
    • Thus
    • So

4、Grammatical

  • Precise 不同种类的时态
  • 复杂句
  • 准确用标点符号
  • 避免简单语法错误

para1 consists of three sentences

  • neural statement to point out the backgroup；
  • paraphrase the topic 1~2 zoom
  • This essay will…

Para 2, 3, (&4)

  • use a paralleled topic sentence to start the paragraph
  • follow P-E-E-P

口语SPEAKING

TASK1

The candidate and the examiner introduce themselves. Candidates then answer general questions about themselves,their home/family, their job/studies, their interests and a wide range of similar familiar topic areas. This part lasts between four and five minutes

  • Some Tricks

    Two sentences: Answer + Explainsion

    One Spirit: With Fluently

TASK2

The candidate is given a task card with prompts and is asked to talk on a particular topic. The candidate has one minute to prepare and they can make some notes if they wish, before speaking for between one and two minutes. The examiner then asks one or two questions on the same topic.

  • Keywords: Vocubary, Expression
  • Answer: Theme + Case
  • 3 Tips:
    • Don’t recite the standard answer
    • No Acadamic or Writing sentence
    • Be careful of Tense, and use different tenses as much as possible

TASK3

The examiner and the candidate engage in a discussion of more abstract issues which are thematically linked to the topic in Part2. The discussion lasts between four and five minutes.

  • Structure
    • Answer
    • explainsion
    • example
    • counter example(optional)
